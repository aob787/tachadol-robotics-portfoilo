Project Overview

Tanuki is a scalable end-effector platform designed for fine manipulation tasks where reliable tool use is required. The concept emphasizes modularity, rapid iteration, and repeatable deployment across different robot configurations.

My Contribution

  • Mechanical design and integration support for tool-ready end-effector modules.
  • Iterative prototyping for robust handling and repeatable operation.
  • Cross-team collaboration for operation readiness and trial execution.
