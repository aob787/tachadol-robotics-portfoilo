## Project Overview

This project investigates a jamming-based wheel mechanism that can actively adjust stiffness to improve mobility on soft terrain. The work focuses on balancing traction, sinkage control, and adaptability under changing terrain conditions.

## Research Context

This project is associated with my master's degree work and thesis:
Design and Development of Jamming-Based Stiffness-Adjustable Wheel on Soft Terrain.

## My Contribution

- Mechanical design and system development of the variable-stiffness wheel concept.
- Prototyping and experimental validation across soft-terrain scenarios.
- Integration of analysis and testing results into thesis and publication outputs.
